World Wide Wii U

Making it possible to play your own real wii u from anywhere


About the Project

This is a project I started as a proof of concept kind of deal, I started working on it, then put it to the side until mid 2024 where I came back to try and build on it, making designs look nicer, better video latency, writing my own audio proccesor to get audio latency as little as possible. Adding touch screen/mobile support, adding nunchuck support and adding motion controller support to make the experience just that bit better to be the cherry on top.

The below screenshot is from 250 miles away from my wii u

I'm hoping that whatever i can make out of this will catch the eye of people who have one laying around and thing "hey this is a cool project, let me donate to help keep it going", I'm just doing what i love to do but to get recognision from what I do would be a dream come true.
