About the Project

I decided to make this project while thinking of a vr game to play, as a kid, i used to go to the slot machines and try so hard to win something even if i never could, but those are the memories that made me feel happy and i want that happiness i did back then (I'll let you know if i ever find it) but because of this I decided to make a start to this project. This is no where near finished...

I'm hoping that whatever i can make out of this will catch the eye of people who have one laying around and thing "hey this is a cool project, let me donate to help keep it going", I'm just doing what i love to do but to get recognision from what I do would be a dream come true.
