Infinity Base Project

Unleashing the Power of Disney Infinity Base with Unreal Engine


About the Project

The Infinity Base Project allows Unreal Engine to control the Disney Infinity Base by sending bulk packets using libusb. This project was something i started in my spare time as I was looking around my room for things I can make into a project, for those who dont know, i keep fear, anger, digust, sadness and joy on my desk, which gave me an idea to start this project.

I'm hoping that whatever i can make out of this will catch the eye of people who have one laying around and thing "hey this is a cool project, let me donate to help keep it going", I'm just doing what i love to do but to get recognision from what I do would be a dream come true.
