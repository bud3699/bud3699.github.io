Hey, I'm Bud. I'm a programmer, game developer, and engineer passionate about creating innovative solutions while having fun. I'm self-taught with extensive experience in reverse engineering using tools like x64dbg, Ghidra, IL2CPP, and DNSpy. I've developed mods for Subnautica, Minecraft (Java & Bedrock), and Rust. I enjoy modifying Unity games' C# assemblies to unlock features or tweak gameplay, or just explore how games are created.



I'm all about good vibes, helping others, and finding wonder in life's simple moments. I strive to stay chill and make the best of every situation.
